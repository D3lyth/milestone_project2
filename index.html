How to Play - Instructions

The aim of the game is to select 5 correct emojis, in the correct order.

  1. Click the New Game button to begin.
  2. Click on the emojis to fill the tiles.
  3. Emojis will only appear ONCE in a sequence.
  4. Click 'Backspace' to delete one emoji or 'Clear' to re-enter the row.
  5. Click 'Guess' to check your answer.
  • If a pink square appears:
  • The emoji is not present in the answer.
  • If a yellow square appears:
  • The emoji is present in the answer, but in the wrong position.
  • If a green square appears:
  • The emoji is in the correct space.

To win - get all emojis in the correct place (all green) in 6 or less guesses.
Good luck!
